About Us

NeuRhône Gallery is a science outreach project that bridges neurosciences and art by showcasing the most striking research visuals from Lyon's neuroscience community.

Each image tells a story — of neurons firing, of brain structures revealed, of data transformed into something beautiful. Behind every image is a researcher who chose it because it captures something special about their work.

This project was born from the idea that scientific imagery can inspire awe and curiosity in anyone, not just scientists. We invite you to explore, learn, and see the brain like you never have before.

Contact

Interested in contributing an image or collaborating? Reach out to us at neurhonegallery@gmail.com

NeuRhône Gallery is based in Lyon, France, within the neuroscience research community of Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1 and associated laboratories.
